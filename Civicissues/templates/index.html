Fixora - Smart Cities Start with Smart Fixes

Fixora is a smart civic issue reporting and prediction platform that connects citizens and government on a real-time heatmap. From potholes to streetlights, it turns complaints into proactive fixes for cleaner, safer cities.

Get Started

Features & Benefits

Dual Portals

Citizens report, government tracks & resolves issues in real time for smarter city management.

Smart Heatmap

Issues appear as red dots that grow with community upvotes, highlighting priority problems visually.

AI Predictions

Anticipates potholes, garbage, and seasonal problems before they occur to enable proactive action.

Proactive Alerts

Citizens & officials get early warnings validated by real users for faster, smarter responses.

Auto-Detection

Sensors & GPS auto-spot potholes/hazards while citizens move, updating the heatmap in real-time.

Proof & Rewards

Before-after photos plus gamified points encourage citizen participation and continuous engagement.
